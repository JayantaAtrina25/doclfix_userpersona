07💡

Key Learnings This Year

CEO POV: A person who reflects and learns is an asset. A person who only executes without learning becomes a liability.
✓
Leadership Grows Through Doing — Not Waiting
Initially hesitant about mentoring and leading sessions. Learned that leadership confidence comes from stepping into the role — not waiting until you feel fully ready.
✓
Asking the Right Questions is a QA Superpower
Through parameter discussions and test planning sessions, understood that asking the right question before testing saves more time than running 100 wrong test cases.
✓
Teaching Deepens Your Own Knowledge
Explaining API testing, automation, and QA processes to others forced a deeper understanding of these topics personally — creating a positive learning loop.
✓
Visibility is as Important as Delivery
Good work that is not tracked does not exist in the eyes of leadership. Going forward, every contribution — mentoring, review, support — will be logged and visible.
