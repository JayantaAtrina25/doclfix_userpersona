07💡

Key Learnings

A person who reflects and learns is a long-term asset. Growth mindset over fixed mindset.
✓
Leadership Grows Through Doing — Not Waiting
Started hesitant about mentoring. Learned that leadership confidence builds from stepping into the role, not waiting until you feel fully ready. Now actively leading team sessions and discussions.
✓
Asking the Right Questions is a QA Superpower
Through test parameter discussions and planning, understood that asking the right question upfront saves more time than running 100 wrong test cases. Structured thinking = better coverage.
✓
Teaching Others Deepens Your Own Knowledge
Explaining API testing, automation, and QA processes to others forced a deeper personal understanding — creating a compounding learning loop that benefits everyone.
✓
Visibility is as Important as Delivery
Good work that is not tracked doesn't exist in the eyes of leadership. Going forward, every contribution — mentoring, review, troubleshooting — will be documented and visible.
